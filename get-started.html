How It Works

Before We Begin: Getting to Know You

The first and most important step isn't about college applications at all—it's about getting to know you as a person. I want to take the college application out of the picture for the moment and just spend time understanding who you are. This is a judgment-free, comfortable space where you can share anything and express yourself genuinely and authentically. I'll ask questions about your memories, meaningful moments in your life, your dreams, fears, and experiences. This essential foundation helps me capture your authentic voice and story before we even think about essays.
